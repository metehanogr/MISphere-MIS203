Welcome to MISphere

MISphere is a classic-style blog and community space for MIS students. Discover course-related posts, upcoming events, and discussions all in one place.

What you can find here

This website is designed as a simple information hub for MIS students. You can follow department events, share ideas in the discussion forum, and keep a basic student profile – all in a clean and minimal layout.

The design focuses on readability and structure rather than complex visuals, using standard HTML, CSS and a small amount of JavaScript.
